Terapanth Bhavan is a magnificent three-storied building over a sprawling 60 acre land situated in the heart of the city which makes it idle for hosting social gatherings, religious conferences and marriage events. It has good connectivity by road and sharing autos are also regularly available at very nominal rates from the nearest railway station. Its centralized location makes it readily accessible to all the prominent market areas.

he first two floor of the community centre serve as guest houses that can accommodate 200 guests. Most of the rooms are air-conditioned and are available on twin sharing basis. Though we also have a provision and arrangement of single bedded rooms. The rooms have a modern interior with all basic facilities included in the its tariff. The booking of the same can be made by filling the form available in service sect of this website. Prior booking is mandatory. We aim to serve the people with an ease on their pockets and have limited availability of the rooms. The third floor has an air conditioned conference hall and an eating area. The conference hall can house a gathering of hundred people at a time and is equipped with projectors and all modern amenities required. Special arrangements (regarding seating arrangement) can be made on request of the guests. Our eating hall can accommodate 50 people at once. We believe in simplicity and oneness with nature which is reflected in our food too. We believe n disciplined life in accordance with our principles of Terapanth .Thus, the eatery is functional during fixed hours.(The schedule is given once you book a room).The open terrace has a garden area which has some exquisite variety of flowers making it one of the most suitable and beautiful location’s to throw one’s marriage and anniversary parties. The complete community centre is surrounded by an alley that is a perfect pathway for morning walks.

Mahapragya Jan Kalyan Kendra is an NGO that was established by eminent group who was firm believer of the principles of Terapanth sect of Svetambara Jainism. The group works for the poor, ailing and social causes. Thus, our endeavour led to the origin of the Mahapragya Jan Kalyan Kendra which aims to serve the needy.We get our major funding from donations and the booking of Terapanth Bhavan.
